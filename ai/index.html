Grounding rule

The grid is mathematical ground at Y = 0. A standing rig must return explicit left and right contact bones at exactly zero and verify them against the six images.

What this enables

A user can send this URL, their scene JSON, and Save Views images to a vision-capable language model. The response has stable IDs and can be corrected over repeated passes.
